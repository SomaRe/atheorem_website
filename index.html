ABOUT

Welcome! What all do you expect? A PROduct, A PROject or A PROgram…These all are there within A THEOREM which has some PRO within it. Concept, Content, Creativity, Innovation, Development and All Elements till final Composition are the eminent and evident spectrums of A Theorem. To deliver the ultimate digital services, television Programming and experiences with 2D, 3D CG works, motion picture production, we are the front runners of Generation Z, bearing the torch of ignited flames shimmering the flakes on to Creativity and Technology. We believe that creating customer-centric experiences based on best practices, market research, mutual Understandings and user testing is the best way to achieve eminence and perfection. Rising the standards of established theorems is what A Theorem aims in the fields of 2D concepts, 3D CG works, Motion Capture, CGFX, Compositing and AV Recording & Editing. Officials have appreciated our work culture and visions many times. We are well regarded for making finest products, providing dependable services and fast to answer questions.

The main motivation to start this production house A THEOREM takes birth from Mr. Arun Kumar Rapolu’s childhood dream. Inspired not only by the animation scores of films but even world class live action films…Arun dreamed on founding his own Production Company. His vision started to take form when he began venturing into CG field. He was trained in Color Chips and worked as Motion Capture Team Lead in Media Mandi International PVT.LTD for half a decade. By that time he worked for many Globally recognized companies like House OF Moves (HOM),Electronics Arts (E.A.), EIDOS, UBI Soft, Perspective studios. The acknowledgment he served to ‘Mirror Mirror’ got him eminence as the best Motion Capture Lead. He also forged strong relationships within the larger animation and production community through his participations in many International Companies. To encompass the radius of his visions he had done Film Making Course in Annapurna International School of film + media and have obtained Diploma in Film Technology from MFTI. Later with all his profound experience start laid foundation to this company as Film Director, Producer and Screen Writer encouraging hundreds of talents and giving opportunities to many young aspirers. Proud of his endeavors and eager to take the next step in his career with A THEOREM.
